## Doomsday scenario simulator
start with a doomsday list
list of opponent decks/archetypes (grab from goldfish?)
per opp archetype need to know lands/threats (only hate cards?)

a scenario is:
* opp deck
* opp cards in hand
* opp threats (damage per turn, hate in play)
* my random board
* my random hand
* my life total
* possibly exile one random blue card

areas for sophistication:
* constrain hands/boards to be more realistic
* assess which hidden interaction a pile beats automatically
* allow tweaking scenarios
* present the whole thing as an interactive webpage with pictures from
  scryfall, etc.

## Doomsday ponder simulator 
a scenario is:
* cantrips in hand
* lands in play
* cards left in deck
* doomsdays left in deck
* cantrips / surveils left in deck

simulate "digging as hard as possible for doomsday" and see odds of finding it
at each duration and expected time to doomsday.
